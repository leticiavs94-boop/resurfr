Stop juggling spreadsheets, text messages, and paper invoices. Resurfr brings every part of your court service business into one clean, professional platform.

Prospect Map

See every tennis court in your county on a live map. Color-coded by surface type. Filter by status, priority, or last contact date.

Client Management

Full history for every client — surface type, last service date, number of courts, contact notes, and job records all in one place.

Smart Service Alerts

Resurfr knows Har-Tru needs annual topdressing and asphalt resurfaces every 5–7 years. Get automatic alerts before clients even call.

Online Payments

Send professional invoices and collect payment online. Track paid, pending, and overdue balances at a glance. No more chasing checks.

Digital Contracts

Create, send, and collect signatures on contracts digitally. Both parties get a signed copy stored securely. Professional from day one.

Job Scheduling

Book jobs, plan your week, and build efficient routes across the county. Always know what's on the calendar and what's coming next.
